KNOW YOUR VOTE

There's no such thing as a vote that doesn't matter
-Barack Obama
Voting is both a right and a privilege. That is why it is so crucial to have up-to-date information about upcoming elections, including who is runninng for which office (and why!), as well as where you can cast your ballot.

That's where we come in. Know Your Vote is an app designed to help you prepare for upcoming elections. Simply enter your address and select the option that suits you: 1) Find Your Local Representatives; 2) See Contested Elections; or 3) Learn More About Where to Vote.

From there, you can see more information about your elected officials and whether their seats are up for election or find out where you can vote, vote early, or drop off your ballot.

We don't store your address anywhere, so you don't have to worry about any privacy issues.
