KNOW YOUR VOTE
There's no such thing as a vote that doesn't matter.
-Barack Obama
Voting is both a right and a privilege. That is why it is so crucial to have up-to-date information about upcoming elections. Who is running? For which office? Why?. Where can you vote?
That's where we come in. Know Your Vote is an app designed to help you prepare for upcoming elections. Simply enter your address and select the option that suits you: 1)Find Your Local Representatives; 2) See Contested Elections; or 3) Learn More About Where to Vote.
You can also find more information about your elected officials and whether their seats are up for election or find out where you can vote, where you can vote early, or drop off your ballot.
We don't store your address anywhere, so you don't have to worry about any privacy issues.
